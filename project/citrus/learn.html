Learn how to peel an orange with Mr.Orange

1. Cut around the middle of the orange rind.

2. Run your thumb around the cut that you made and try to separate the rind.

3. Once you are done with step 2, the rind should be separated, remove it.

4. Repeat step 2 and 3 on the other side and you should be good!
